Guidelines for Presenters

Presenters are requested to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Keep presentations within the allotted time to allow for a smooth schedule.
  • Use visual aids, such as slides or multimedia, to enhance the clarity and engagement of your presentation.
  • Ensure that your content is clear, concise, and well-organized to facilitate understanding by the audience.
  • Practice your presentation in advance to familiarize yourself with the material and to ensure a confident delivery.
  • Be prepared to answer questions during the Q&A session, and encourage interactive discussions with the audience.
  • Respect the time allocated for Q&A, allowing for diverse perspectives and insights to be shared.
  • Provide a brief overview of your background and expertise at the beginning of your presentation.
  • Consider the diverse background of the audience and tailor your content accordingly, avoiding overly technical jargon.
  • Engage with the audience by encouraging questions, comments, and discussions to create an interactive and dynamic session.
  • Be mindful of the allocated time for your presentation and ensure a smooth transition to the next presenter.
